July 20, 2017:
After going to the Denver Botanical Garden, I have quite a few photos to sort through and to edit. The sun was incredibly bright, so there are many shadows on a few of the photos. However, I feel that these add to the photos rather than detract, and therefore editing the photos should not be difficult.

I am planning a trip soon into Estes Park in order to take some pictures there. I have not been to Rocky Mountain National Park yet, even though it is practically down the road from where I am staying in Colorado. The forest is so dense that I do not think it will be difficult to find good photographs, and maybe an elk or two.

Stay tuned for more updates soon!
