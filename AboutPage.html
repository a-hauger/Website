About the Photographer



Bio:

Anthony is currently a student of Computational Math and Computer Science at the University of Colorado Boulder. He's been studying the past three years.
He bought a kit camera before studying abroad in Italy and has found a passion in photography. He enjoys taking landscape portraits and is learning proficiency in portrait photography. He particularly enjoys taking photographs with action, color and drama.
